Our Vision

We believe that every employee deserves to be treated with respect, no matter their position. At Air Canada, we always try to listen to our employees, and see things from their point-of-view. With input from our current employees, we do our best to improve our employees' experience working at Air Canada, so our employees have the rights they deserve when working for us. We wish to continue creating good work enviroments for our employees, so our employees enjoy working for us.

Why Work at Air Canada?

We always try to do our best to allow optimum employees satisfaction, when working for us. A few of the benefits of working at Air Canada are:

  • Connect with new people everyday
  • Gain experience working in a diverse field of work
  • Learn more about the world and the many cultures around us
  • Extensive training programs
  • Meet mentors and teachers
  • Freedom to voice opinions
  • Employee discounts
